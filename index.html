TEXT STYLE
The text styles determine the style of the text. I show you my styles of headings, articles and paragraphs.

Headings

  • font-family: 'Libre Baskerville', sans-serif;
  • Font-weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 26px
Paragraphs
  • font-family: 'Merriweather', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;
  • font-size: 18px;
  • Font-weight: 400 (regular)

Tables

  • font-family: 'Merriweather', serif;
  • font-size: 18px;
  • line-height: 1.4;
  • text-align: center;
